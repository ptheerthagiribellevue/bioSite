Tansi's hobbies have shaped her character and influenced her long-term goals. Karate has instilled leadership qualities, while swimming has fostered resilience. Reading has enhanced her communication and critical thinking skills.

Beyond personal growth, Tansi actively engages in social activities related to her hobbies, fostering a sense of community. Join her on her inspiring journey and discover the incredible impact of hobbies in shaping lives.
